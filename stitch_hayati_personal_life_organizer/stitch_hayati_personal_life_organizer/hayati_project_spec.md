# Hayati | حياتي - Project Specification

## Overview
A personal life organizer app with bilingual support (Arabic/English) and RTL capabilities.

## Screen List
1. **Landing/Browse**: Features overview, Auth triggers, read-only demo access.
2. **Sign In**: Standard email auth + Google SSO.
3. **Create Account**: Onboarding flow with email verification (OTP).
4. **Dashboard**: Main overview with metrics (tasks, goals, study, streak), mood chart, and health tracking.
5. **To-Do List**: Dual-mode (Time-based with Salah slots vs. Eisenhower Matrix).
6. **Goals & Future Plans**: Motivational UI with progress tracking and vision statement.
7. **Courses & Videos**: Learning management with Pomodoro integration.
8. **Pomodoro Timer**: Functional countdown with session logging.
9. **Daily Mood Log**: Daily check-in modal.

## Visual Identity

- **Accent**: #6366F1 (Indigo)
